About

Built by people who were tired of cloud AI promises

Manager OS started with a simple observation: every AI "solution" wanted your data in their cloud. We thought that was backwards.

So we built the opposite - AI that lives in your office, works offline, and keeps your data where it belongs. No APIs routing your client information through third-party servers. No subscriptions that grow faster than the value they deliver.

We deploy physical Mac Mini stations to UK businesses. Real hardware. Real AI. Real privacy. The kind of setup that makes your IT team nod approvingly instead of raising red flags.

Why Physical

Why physical hardware matters

Privacy by Design

No API calls. No data transmission. No third-party access. Your data stays on your hardware, full stop.

Reliability

Works without internet. When cloud services go down, your AI keeps running. No outages, no disruptions.

Speed

Local processing beats cloud latency every time. Your queries are answered instantly, not queued behind thousands of other users.

Ownership

You own the hardware. You own the data. You own the AI. No vendor lock-in, no rented infrastructure.
