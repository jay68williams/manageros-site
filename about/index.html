ABOUT US

We believe businesses should own their AI

Manager OS is a UK-based AI infrastructure company. We deploy physical AI stations to offices. No cloud, no lock-in, no compromise on privacy.

OUR STORY

Why we started Manager OS

We saw a gap in the market. Every AI company was building cloud-first, SaaS-first products. They were asking businesses to hand over their most sensitive data to third-party servers, and pay monthly for the privilege.

We took a different approach. What if AI could run locally? What if businesses could own their AI hardware? What if your data never had to leave your building?

That's Manager OS. A dedicated AI station configured with enterprise-grade AI models, delivered to your office, plugged in, and running. No APIs. No cloud. No per-seat pricing that climbs forever.

HARDWARE FIRST

Why physical hardware matters

Cloud AI is convenient, but convenience comes at a cost. Your data on someone else's servers. Unpredictable pricing. Downtime when their infrastructure fails.

Physical hardware changes the equation entirely:

  • Your data never leaves your network
  • No API rate limits or usage caps
  • Works offline, no internet dependency
  • Predictable, fixed monthly cost
  • You own the hardware after 24 months
  • Full audit trail for compliance
